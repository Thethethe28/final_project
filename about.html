About us

Mangas and animes, everyone knows what it is. There are those who think that they are made only for children, and those who think that children and adults can find comfort in mangas and animes. For us, mangas and animes are not just entertainment, but a whole universe in which one can find refuge because there are some for all tastes and they deal with all subjects.

Our store was founded in 2017 by Mr. KOUADIO YANIS and aims to provide anime lovers and otakus with items with which they will be comfortable in the world of animes such as collectibles, figurines, mangas ... Everything an anime fan needs can be found with us.

The Y Store is here to serve you and meet your needs.
